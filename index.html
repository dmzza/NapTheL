Swipe In, Sit Down, and Tune Out the L train

Tune Out is a nifty lifehack for the subway. It will alert you when your stop is approaching so that you can focus on reading, napping, gaming, doodling, and daydreaming while you ride.
Works underground!

  • Select the stops you are leaving from and going to.
  • Start the clock when the doors close.
  • Make sure your sound is on. The mute switch or do-not-disturb mode will silence the alarm.
  • Works best while listening to music.
  • Only for the NYC MTA L train at this time.
