Tune Out






Tune Out is a nifty lifehack for Brooklyn's L train.

It will alert you when your stop is approaching
so that you can focus on reading, napping, gaming,
doodling, and daydreaming while you ride.

  • Select the stops you are leaving from and going to.
  • Start the clock when the doors close.
  • Make sure your sound is on. The mute switch or do-not-disturb mode will silence the alarm.
  • Works best while listening to music.
