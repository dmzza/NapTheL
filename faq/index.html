Frequently Asked Questions

When should I start the clock?

When the doors close at the station you are leaving from.

What if I forget to start the clock until after the doors close?

The safest thing is to choose the next station as your starting point.
Protip: If you're in a rush, you can start the clock before you select stations.

Why didn't I hear the alert?

You either had your volume too low or the ringer switch on silent. Do-not-disturb mode will also silence the alert.

Why only the L train?

The most important feature of Tune Out is that you will never miss your stop. The L train is the most consistent subway line. Contact me if you are interested in beta testing Tune Out for the other lines.
